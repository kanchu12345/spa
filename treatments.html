Our Treatments

Conditions We Treat

At Shanthi Weda Madhura, Dr. Mangalika Rajasekara applies over two decades of Hela Wedakama expertise to treat a broad spectrum of chronic, acute, and systemic conditions. Every treatment plan is uniquely prescribed after a thorough personal consultation — combining freshly prepared herbal medicines, traditional Shanthi Wedakama rituals, and lifestyle guidance tailored to the individual patient.

01
🦴

Arthritis & Joint Care

ආතරයිටිස් හා සන්ධි රෝග · மூட்டு வாதம்

Chronic arthritis, osteoarthritis, cartilage degeneration, and persistent joint pain are treated with medicated herbal oil applications, internal herbal decoctions, and traditional Hela Wedakama joint therapies. The treatment reduces inflammation, lubricates joints, and restores mobility over a prescribed course.

02
❤️

Cardiovascular & High Blood Pressure

අධි රුධිර පීඩනය · உயர் இரத்த அழுத்தம்

High blood pressure, fatty heart, high cholesterol, and poor circulation are addressed using traditional heart-strengthening herbal formulas derived from Sri Lankan flora. These preparations help regulate blood pressure, improve cardiac function, and reduce arterial inflammation naturally, without the side effects of chemical medications.

03
🔥

Gastritis & Digestive Disorders

ගැස්ට්‍රයිටිස් · இரைப்பை அழற்சி

Chronic gastritis, peptic ulcers, acid reflux, irritable bowel, and all digestive imbalances are treated using soothing herbal decoctions that neutralise excess acid, heal the gastric lining, and restore healthy digestive fire (agni). Dietary advice specific to the patient's constitution is also provided.

04
🚶

Paralysis & Nerve Disorders

අංශභාග · பக்கவாதம்

Hemiplegia (one-sided paralysis), facial palsy, and other neurological conditions are managed through specialised Hela Wedakama nerve therapies — including medicated steam treatments, internal herbal preparations, and targeted oil therapies — to restore nerve signal pathways and recover motor function progressively.

05
🌬️

Sinusitis & Respiratory Conditions

පිනස හා සෙම · சைனஸ் மற்றும் சுவாசம்

Chronic sinusitis, nasal polyps, chronic cough, bronchitis, and respiratory ailments are treated with traditional Hela Wedakama nasal therapies (nasya), herbal steam inhalations, and internal medicines that clear blockages, reduce inflammation in the respiratory tract, and strengthen lung capacity.

06
🧠

Headaches & Migraines

හිස කැක්කුම · தலைவலி மற்றும் ஒற்றைத் தலைவலி

Chronic headaches, cluster headaches, and debilitating migraines are treated with traditional head therapies including medicated oil head massage (shirodhara-style treatments), herbal nasal drops, and internal preparations that calm the nervous system, improve cerebral circulation, and eliminate the root cause of recurring pain.

07
💧

Diabetes & Blood Sugar Management

මධු මේහය · நீரிழிவு நோய்

Both Type 1 and Type 2 diabetes are approached holistically — with specific herbal formulas from Sri Lankan plants known to regulate insulin sensitivity and blood sugar levels. Treatment also addresses diabetic complications such as peripheral neuropathy, poor wound healing, and fatigue through systemic herbal protocols.

08
👶

Children's Fever & Paediatric Care

ළදරු රත රෝග · குழந்தை நோய்கள்

Gentle, child-safe traditional herbal remedies are prescribed for childhood fever diseases (rata roga), digestive issues, recurring infections, and developmental conditions. All medicines are prepared fresh with mild Sri Lankan herbs, carefully dosed for the child's age and weight.

09
💪

Rheumatic & Vata Diseases

වාත රෝග · வாத நோய்கள்

Rheumatoid arthritis, gout, muscle stiffness, and vata-imbalance disorders are treated with warming herbal oils, anti-inflammatory decoctions, and detoxification therapies that eliminate accumulated toxins (ama) from the body and restore the balance of the three doshas.

10
🎗️

Cancer & Tumour Support Care

පිළිකා සහාය · புற்றுநோய் ஆதரவு

While not a replacement for allopathic oncology, Hela Wedakama offers powerful complementary support — using immune-boosting herbal compounds, detoxifying preparations, and Shanthi Wedakama spiritual healing to strengthen the body, improve quality of life, and support recovery during or after conventional cancer treatment.

11
🦠

Mumps, Skin Infections & Viral Conditions

මූනු ගල් · காஞ்சாமாடு

Traditional Hela Wedakama has highly effective treatments for viral infections including mumps, chickenpox, and skin-related infections. Specific anti-viral herbal preparations and topical applications derived from Sri Lankan plant medicine reduce duration, severity, and recurrence of infections.

12
🌟

General Systemic Wellness

සර්වාංග රෝග · பொது ஆரோக்கியம்

For patients suffering from multiple systemic complaints without a clear single diagnosis, Dr. Rajasekara conducts a full constitutional assessment and prescribes a comprehensive Hela Wedakama programme — targeting the root imbalance causing widespread symptoms throughout the body.

13
🧘

Stress, Anxiety & Mental Fatigue

මානසික ආතතිය · மன அழுத்தம்

Chronic stress, anxiety disorders, insomnia, and mental exhaustion are treated through Shanthi Wedakama — combining medicated calming herbal preparations, herbal oil head therapies, and traditional spiritual rituals that soothe the nervous system and restore psychological balance and mental clarity.

14
🦷

Oral Health & Dental Conditions

දන්ත රෝග · பல் நோய்கள்

Gum disease, toothache, mouth ulcers, and oral infections are treated with traditional herbal mouth washes, astringent plant preparations, and anti-inflammatory compounds used in Sri Lankan Hela Wedakama for centuries to maintain strong teeth and healthy gum tissue.

15
👁️

Eye Diseases & Vision Problems

ඇස් රෝග · கண் நோய்கள்

Conjunctivitis, dry eye syndrome, weakening vision, and eye fatigue are addressed using traditional Sri Lankan herbal eye drops (anjanam), internal vision-strengthening preparations, and detoxification therapies that cleanse the body systems linked to ocular health.

16
🩸

Anaemia & Blood Disorders

රක්තහීනතාවය · இரத்த சோகை

Iron-deficiency anaemia, thalassaemia support, and general blood weakness are treated with haematinic herbal preparations rich in natural iron and blood-building compounds found in Sri Lankan medicinal flora — strengthening haemoglobin levels and restoring vitality naturally.

17
🌸

Women's Health & Gynaecological Conditions

කාන්තා රෝග · பெண் நோய்கள்

Menstrual irregularities, PCOS, leucorrhea, menopausal discomfort, and hormonal imbalances are treated with specialised Hela Wedakama women's formulas — gentle yet potent herbal preparations that regulate the female reproductive system and promote hormonal balance without synthetic hormones.

18
🫁

Asthma & Chronic Lung Conditions

ඇදුම · ஆஸ்துமா

Bronchial asthma, chronic obstructive pulmonary conditions, and recurring breathlessness are managed through bronchodilatory herbal preparations, steam therapies with medicated herbs, and internal lung-strengthening compounds that progressively reduce dependency on inhalers and improve respiratory function.

19
🫀

Kidney & Urinary Disorders

වකුගඩු රෝග · சிறுநீரக நோய்கள்

Kidney stones, urinary tract infections, poor kidney function, and prostate conditions are addressed with traditional diuretic and nephro-protective herbal formulas. These preparations cleanse the urinary tract, dissolve calculi, reduce inflammation, and support long-term kidney health.

20
🧬

Thyroid Disorders

තයිරොයිඩ් රෝග · தைராய்டு நோய்கள்

Both hypothyroidism and hyperthyroidism are approached through traditional Hela Wedakama endocrine-balancing protocols — using specific herbal preparations that regulate thyroid hormone production, reduce goitre, improve metabolism, and alleviate associated symptoms such as fatigue, weight changes, and mood swings.

21
🦵

Sciatica & Back Pain

ලිහිල් ස්නායු රෝග · இடுப்பு வலி

Sciatica, lumbar disc disease, chronic lower back pain, and spinal conditions are treated with targeted medicated herbal oil therapies, warming poultice applications, and internal preparations that reduce nerve inflammation, rebuild disc health, and restore spinal alignment and mobility.

22
🌿

Skin Diseases & Dermatological Conditions

සමේ රෝග · தோல் நோய்கள்

Psoriasis, eczema, chronic urticaria, vitiligo, and persistent skin conditions are treated with blood-purifying herbal preparations, traditional topical applications, and internal cleansing compounds that eliminate the systemic toxins causing skin manifestations — addressing the disease from within rather than only the surface.

23
🛌

Insomnia & Sleep Disorders

නිදා නොගැනීම · தூக்கமின்மை

Chronic insomnia, disrupted sleep patterns, and sleep disorders are treated through Shanthi Wedakama calming rituals combined with sedative herbal preparations from Sri Lankan plants — helping patients achieve deep, restorative natural sleep without dependence on pharmaceutical sleep aids.

24
✨

Post-Illness Recovery & Rejuvenation

රෝගෙන් සුවය ලැබීම · நோய்க்கு பின் மீட்சி

Patients recovering from surgery, long-term illness, or post-COVID conditions benefit greatly from Hela Wedakama rejuvenation protocols (rasayana) — a comprehensive programme using tonic herbal preparations, body-strengthening oils, nutritional guidance, and Shanthi Wedakama practices to fully restore vitality, immunity, and energy.
